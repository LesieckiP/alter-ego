My name is Piotr Lesiecki. I'm a Quality Engineer, Senior SDET, and competitive age-group triathlete based in Poland. I've spent over 15 years building high-quality software — crafting automation frameworks, shaping QA strategies, and leading engineering teams. Sport has been woven into my life since childhood, when my dad signed me up for my first races before I was even old enough to join a club. That early spark never left — after years of focusing on career and family, I came back to competition and somehow ended up on the Ironman European Championship podium and brought home national titles as an Age Grouper. Away from the finish line and the code, I'm a proud and happy father of two wonderful kids — a boy and a girl — who remind me every day why discipline, consistency, and joy in the process matter more than any trophy.
